Lopzan Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name chosen is "Lopzan Chamber of Commerce". This name reflects the regional focus of the chamber, specifically serving the Tulsa area.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Lopzan Chamber of Commerce website is to offer information about local events, attractions, and community initiatives. The website aims to:

  • Facilitate networking and collaboration among local businesses.
  • Provide resources and support for entrepreneurs and small business owners.
  • Promote economic development and growth in the Tulsa area.
  • Offer information about local events, attractions, and community initiatives.
  • How can I find small and new interesting businesses in the area?

Scenarios

These scenarios represent typical questions or situations that visitors to the site may have:

  1. What community events are scheduled for this weekend in Tulsa?
  2. Where can I find information about art and cultural festivals taking place in Tulsa this month?
  3. What are the main tourist attractions recommended by the Chamber of Commerce in Tulsa?
  4. How can I get involved in community initiatives to improve my neighborhood in Tulsa?
  5. How can I find small and new interesting businesses in the area?

Color Schema

The color scheme of the Lopzan Chamber of Commerce website is designed to reflect the vibrant and welcoming atmosphere of Tulsa. The following colors will be used:

Main Colors:

  • Primary Color: #2E86C1 (Blue) - This color will be used for headings, buttons, and important accents throughout the site.
  • Secondary Color: #F39C12 (Yellow) - This color will be used for highlights, call-to-action elements, and visual accents.
  • Tertiary Color: #8BC34A (Light Green) - This light green color will be used sparingly for backgrounds and other subtle elements.

Typography

The typography of the Lopzan Chamber of Commerce website is carefully chosen to reflect professionalism and readability. The following fonts will be used:

Main Fonts:

  • Heading Font: "Roboto", sans-serif - This font will be used for headings and titles throughout the site for its clean and modern appearance.
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif - This font will be used for body text and paragraphs for its legibility and versatility.
